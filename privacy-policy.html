Privacy Policy for Screen Recorder & SOP Generator

Last updated: January 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Screen Recorder & SOP Generator ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Screen Recording Data: Screenshots and screen recordings that you choose to capture
  • User Actions: Information about the steps you perform during SOP creation
  • Website Information: URL and basic page information for the websites you choose to record
  • Settings and Preferences: Your extension settings and configuration preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Creating screen recordings and SOP documentation
  • Storing your preferences and settings locally
  • Providing the core functionality of the extension

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • No External Servers: We do not transmit your data to external servers or third parties
  • User Control: You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

6. Permissions Explanation

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab for screen recording
  • storage: To save your settings and recordings locally
  • scripting: To inject necessary scripts for recording functionality
  • tabs: To interact with browser tabs during recording

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review any data stored by the extension
  • Delete your data at any time through the extension interface
  • Disable or uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored data

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.
